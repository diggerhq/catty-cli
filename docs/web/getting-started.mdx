---
title: "Getting Started"
description: "Connect to a session from your browser in 30 seconds"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open app.catty.dev">
    Go to [app.catty.dev](https://app.catty.dev) on your phone or any browser. Sign in with the same account you use for the CLI.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Tap a Session">
    Your running sessions appear on the dashboard. Tap one to connect.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your View">
    - **Chat Mode** (default on mobile) - Clean chat bubbles with quick action buttons. Best for phone.
    - **Terminal Mode** - Full terminal emulator. Best for tablets and desktops.

    Switch anytime using the toggle in the header.
  </Step>
</Steps>

That's it. You're now connected to your Claude Code session from anywhere.
